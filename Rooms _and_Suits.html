Luxurious Rooms & Suites

Indulge in the ultimate comfort and elegance in our beautifully designed rooms and suites at My Dream Hotel. Each space is a sanctuary, meticulously crafted to provide a restful retreat. Enjoy plush bedding, modern amenities, and stunning views that invite relaxation and tranquility. Whether you choose a cozy standard room or a spacious suite, you’ll find a perfect blend of style and functionality. Experience personalized service and thoughtful details that make your stay unforgettable. Discover a home away from home where every moment is designed for your comfort.

Standard Room

A cozy and comfortable space equipped with essential amenities, ideal for solo travelers or couples. The standard room typically features a queen or king-sized bed, a private bathroom, and basic furnishings, providing a welcoming environment for a restful stay.

  • 16 M² space
  • Air conditioner
  • Queen size bed
  • Free breakfast

Deluxe Room

Experience enhanced comfort in our deluxe rooms, which offer more space and upgraded amenities. These rooms often include premium bedding, a seating area, and a larger bathroom. Perfect for those seeking a touch of luxury without breaking the bank.

  • 30 M² space
  • Air conditioner
  • Queen size bed
  • Free wifi
  • Free breakfast

Suite

Our spacious suites are designed for those who appreciate extra room and elegance. Featuring a separate living area, a bedroom, and a luxurious bathroom, these accommodations provide an ideal setting for families or guests looking to enjoy a more lavish experience. Some suites may also offer kitchen facilities and stunning views.

  • 30 M² space
  • Air conditioner
  • Queen size bed
  • Free wifi
  • Free breakfast

Executive Room

Tailored for business travelers, our executive rooms combine style with functionality. These rooms often include a large workspace, high-speed internet access, and additional services like complimentary breakfast or access to a business lounge. Enjoy a peaceful atmosphere that caters to your professional needs while ensuring your comfort.

  • 30 M² space
  • Air conditioner
  • Queen size bed
  • Free wifi
  • Free breakfast
